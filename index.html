Getting Started
How to find things and how every HR form works — read this once and the rest is easy.

You work in the Desk — the web interface at your company's ERP address (e.g. your-company.enfonoerp.com/app). HR lives in two workspaces on the left sidebar: HR and Payroll.

Finding a screen — 3 ways

Search

Awesomebar

Press Ctrl/Cmd + G or click the top search bar, type e.g. "Leave Application", hit Enter.

Sidebar

Workspace

Click HR or Payroll on the left, then a shortcut or card.

URL

Direct link

Every list is /app/<doctype> — e.g. /app/employee. New record: add /new.

How every form works (the universal flow)

Almost everything in HR is a document you create, save, and (for approvals/payroll) submit. Learn this once:

1

Open the list and click the blue + Add button (top-right) to start a new record.

2

Fill the fields. Anything with a red asterisk * is mandatory. Link fields (with a magnifier) let you search existing records.

3

Save with Ctrl/Cmd + S (or the Save button). The badge changes from Not Saved to Saved.

4

Submit (for approval / payroll documents) to lock and action it. A submitted doc can only be changed by Amend (cancel → new copy).

Status badges you'll see everywhere: Draft (grey) → Submitted/Open → Approved/Completed (green) or Rejected/Cancelled (red).
Employee & Lifecycle
Add people, then manage every event from joining to exit.

How to add an employee

1

Go to HR › Employee (or search "Employee") and click + Add Employee.

2

On the Overview tab fill the essentials: First Name, Gender, Date of Birth, Date of Joining.

3

Under Company Details pick the Company, and set Designation, Department, Branch, and Reports to (the manager).

4

Open the other tabs as needed — Joining, Address & Contacts, Attendance & Leaves (Holiday List, Leave Approver), Salary — then Save.

5

To let them self-serve (and use Fateh HR), set the User ID on the employee to their login email.

FieldWhat to enter
First Name *Employee's given name (Middle / Last optional).
Date of Joining *Drives leave allocation, payroll start, and tenure.
Company *Which company they belong to.
Reports toTheir manager — used for approvals and the org chart.
Holiday ListWeekly-offs & public holidays (Attendance & Leaves tab).
User IDLinks a login so the person can self-serve / use the app.

Lifecycle events

Onboarding→Active→Transfer / Promotion→Separation

Use Employee Onboarding to run a joining checklist that can auto-create the employee. Later, record Employee Transfer (department/branch change), Employee Promotion (designation/grade change), and finally Employee Separation (exit checklist & relieving). Each is a document under the HR workspace — create, fill the effective date, submit.

Leave Management
Set up leave once, allocate it, then employees apply and balances update automatically.
Leave Type→Leave Policy→Allocation→Application

Step 1 — Define Leave Types

Go to HR › Leave Type › + Add. Give it a name (e.g. Annual Leave) and set the rules: max days allowed, whether it's paid, carry-forward, and whether it's encashable.

Step 2 — Allocate leave to employees

Employees can only apply once they have a balance. Either assign a Leave Policy (a bundle of types + annual counts) via a Leave Policy Assignment, or create a Leave Allocation directly for an employee and period.

Balance showing 0? There's no Leave Allocation for that employee & type yet — that's the #1 cause.

Step 3 — Apply for leave

The employee (or HR on their behalf) goes to HR › Leave Application › + Add, picks the Employee, Leave Type and From/To dates. The form shows the available balance. Submit sends it to the Leave Approver; on approval the balance is reduced.

Holidays

Create a Holiday List (HR › Holiday List) for the year — add weekly-offs in bulk and public holidays. Assign it on each Employee so attendance and leave know their non-working days.

Attendance & Shifts
Mark attendance by hand, or let shifts turn check-ins into attendance automatically.

How to mark attendance manually

Go to HR › Attendance › + Add, choose the Employee and Date, set Status (Present / Absent / On Leave / Half Day), then Submit. For a whole team at once, use the Attendance list › Mark Attendance tool.

How to automate attendance with Shifts

1

Create a Shift Type (HR › Shift Type) — set start/end time and grace periods.

2

Turn on Enable Auto Attendance and set how check-ins map to Present/Absent (and when it runs).

3

Put employees on the shift with a Shift Assignment. Their Employee Checkins (from Fateh HR or a device) then become daily Attendance automatically.

This is the backbone of the Fateh HR field check-in flow — staff tap Check In on the app, the shift's Auto Attendance does the rest.
Expense Claims & Advances
Reimburse staff, with approval and a clean posting to accounts.
Draft→Submitted→Approved→Paid

How to file an expense claim

1

Go to HR › Expense Claim › + Add and select the Employee.

2

In Expenses, add a row per item — Expense Type, Amount, date and description. Attach the receipt.

3

Set the Expense Approver and Submit. Once approved, it's paid via a Payment Entry.

Employees can file claims (with a receipt photo per line) straight from Fateh HR. Use Employee Advance for money given up front, and Travel Request for pre-trip approval.
Payroll
Build the pay structure once, then run payroll every month in a few clicks.
Salary Components→Salary Structure→Assignment→Payroll Entry→Salary Slips

Step 1 — Create Salary Components

Each earning or deduction is a Salary Component (Payroll › Salary Component) — e.g. Basic, Housing Allowance, GOSI, Loan. Set whether it's an earning or deduction and, if variable, a formula or condition.

Step 2 — Build a Salary Structure

A Salary Structure (Payroll › Salary Structure) bundles the components into a reusable template — list the earnings and deductions and how each is calculated.

Step 3 — Assign & run payroll

1

Assign the structure to each employee with a Salary Structure Assignment (their base pay + from date).

2

Create a Payroll Entry for the period, pick the company/branch/department filter, and pull the employees.

3

Click Create Salary Slips → review → Submit. Each employee gets a Salary Slip they can download (and view in Fateh HR).

Statutory & regional

Model deductions like GOSI (KSA) or end-of-service as Salary Components with the right formula/caps. Confirm current rates with the client's payroll lead before go-live.

Performance
Run appraisal cycles against KRAs, with self- and manager scoring.

How to run an appraisal cycle

1

Create an Appraisal Template with the KRAs / rating criteria and their weightings.

2

Create an Appraisal Cycle (Performance › Appraisal Cycle) for the review period, add the employees, and generate their appraisals.

3

Employees self-score their Goals; managers score and finalise. The cycle rolls up the ratings.

Recruitment
From an open vacancy to a signed offer — then straight into onboarding.
Job Opening→Applicant→Interview→Job Offer

How to post a job & track applicants

1

Create a Job Opening (Recruitment › Job Opening) — tie it to a Designation and Department, set the status to Open.

2

Log each candidate as a Job Applicant against the opening; track their Status as they progress.

3

Schedule Interviews, record feedback, then raise a Job Offer. On acceptance, flow the candidate into Employee Onboarding.

Roles & Settings
Who can do what, and where the global switches live.
RoleTypical access
HR ManagerFull HR — masters, policies, payroll, approvals, settings.
HR UserDay-to-day HR operations; usually not payroll finalisation.
EmployeeSelf-service — own profile, leave, claims, payslips (the Fateh HR surface).
ApproversLeave / Expense Approver — set per employee (or by department).

Global switches live in HR Settings. Assign Holiday Lists and Approvers on each Employee (Attendance & Leaves tab). To give someone access, an Administrator adds the right Role to their User.

Rollout Checklist
The Enfono order for standing up HR for a new client.
  1. Company, departments, designations, grades created.
  2. Holiday List(s) built and assigned to employees.
  3. Employees created (or imported) with joining date, manager, User ID.
  4. Leave Types + Leave Policy defined; allocations run so balances exist.
  5. Shift Type(s) with Auto Attendance; Shift Assignments for shift staff.
  6. Expense Claim Types mapped to accounts; approvers set.
  7. Salary Components + Structures built; Assignments done; statutory (e.g. GOSI) confirmed.
  8. Roles assigned (HR Manager / HR User / Employee); approvers per employee.
  9. Test one full cycle: check-in → attendance → leave → expense → payroll → payslip.
  10. Roll out Fateh HR — share the app link, brief staff on login → PIN → check-in.
